AquaFeeder Machine

AquaFeeder Machine is an IoT-enabled smart aquaculture system developed to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of fish farming operations. Powered by an ESP32 microcontroller, the system serves as a centralized platform for automation, real-time monitoring, and wireless communication.

Beyond automated feeding, the AquaFeeder Machine is equipped with multiple sensors that continuously assess essential water quality parameters, including temperature, pH level, turbidity, and total dissolved solids (TDS). The ESP32 processes the collected sensor data and enables it to be viewed remotely through a monitoring interface. When water conditions deviate from the optimal range for fish pond environments, the system can trigger specific actuators or send notifications to users for immediate action. By combining intelligent monitoring with automated control, the AquaFeeder Machine offers a reliable and innovative solution for improving fish farm management practices.
